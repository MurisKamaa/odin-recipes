Preparation of African Tea

Description

African tea is a popular drink in most Kenyan households.

It's prepared using milk and may be taken with or without sugar

African tea is best served hot


Ingridients

  • 1/4 liter milk
  • 1/8 liter water
  • 1/2 teaspoon tea leaves
  • 2 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of tea masala

Steps

  1. Put the milk in a cooking pan and allow it to boil
  2. Add the water
  3. Add the tea leaves and tea masala
  4. Let it boil
  5. Add the sugar if needed
  6. Boil for another 3-5 minutes
  7. Sieve the tea and enjoy your drink
