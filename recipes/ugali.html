Preparation of Ugali

Description

Ugali is a popular dish in sub-saharan Africa, especially Kenya.

It's made from maize (corn) flour

It's normally served with kales and fried meat

It's very easy to prepare Ugali and it's less time consuming


Ingridients

  • Maize flour
  • Water
  • VWooden spoon
  • Cooking pot

Steps

  1. Set the heat to moderate
  2. Add water to a pot and bring it to boil
  3. Once the water has boiled add the flour in potions mixing it to make a paste
  4. Mix the water and flour vigorously using the wooden spoon
  5. Cover the pot and reduce the heat to ensure that the meal is well cooked
  6. The ugali is ready
  7. Enjoy with kales and fried meat
